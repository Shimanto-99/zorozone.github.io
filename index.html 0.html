About Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is one of the main protagonists of the series and a member of the Straw Hat Pirates, the crew led by the main character, Monkey D. Luffy. Zoro is a skilled swordsman and is often regarded as one of the most powerful fighters in the series.Overall, Roronoa Zoro is a fan-favorite character in the One Piece series, admired for his impressive combat skills, unwavering loyalty, and distinct personality.
